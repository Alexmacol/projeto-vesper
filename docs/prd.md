# Project Brief: Studio Vesper Landing Page

## 1. Project Overview
**Client:** Alex Macol, Founder of Studio Vesper
**Industry:** High-end Interior Design & Architecture
**Objective:** Create an elegant, sophisticated landing page that serves as an immersive experience, reflecting a "quiet luxury" aesthetic for a São Paulo-based boutique studio.

## 2. Brand Identity & Visual Direction
**Design System:** Vesper Obscura
**Aesthetic:** Boutique hotel, art gallery, minimalist luxury.
**Color Palette:** 
- Primary: Deep Charcoal / Midnight Void (`#121414`, `#00081c`)
- Accent: Brass/Gold (`#AF9461`)
- Text: Gallery White / Muted Silver
**Typography:** 
- Headings: *Bodoni Moda* (Serif, editorial style)
- Body: *Montserrat* (Clean Sans-Serif, modern, legible)

## 3. Core Requirements & Features
- **Hero Section:** Immersive opening with a background video (`interior-dark-video.mp4`).
- **Process Section:** Three-step work methodology featuring a process video (`video-camadas.mp4`).
- **Portfolio:** A curated gallery showcasing 7 residential and corporate projects.
- **Founder Profile:** "About" section featuring Alex Macol’s history and philosophy.
- **Social Proof:** Client testimonials ("Voices of Our Clients").
- **Conversion:** Contact form for proposals and a direct WhatsApp integration.
- **Navigation:** Transparent fixed header with links to Process, Portfolio, About, and Contact.

## 4. Asset Inventory
- **Video:** `interior-dark-video.mp4` (Hero), `video-camadas.mp4` (Process).
- **Images:** 7 high-resolution interior project photos.
- **Copy:** Content focused on timelessness, precision, and sensory delivery.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Device Support:** Desktop and Mobile (Responsive).
- **Technology:** HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla), Vanilla JavaScript.
- **Interactions:** Subtle transitions, backdrop blurs, and hover effects on interactive elements.
